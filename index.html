Welcome

Cuts-4-Muttz was founded in 2002 by owner Bridget Behrens, who completed her training from the Animal Institute in Winter Park, FL in 1998. Bridget believes in treating ALL dogs with respect and gentleness, which has led to the success of Cuts-4-Muttz in Wayne County.

Cuts-4-Muttz is proud to use only professional grade products for the bathing and grooming of your dog. Our staff is well-trained and closely supervised too provide the highest quality care for Man’s (or Women’s) Best Friend!

While specializing in all breeds of dogs, we are unable to groom cats or other small pets, lizards, or small children.

Cuts-4-Muttz is fully insured and licensed with the State of North Carolina under all applicable statutes and regulations.
